> YuumiBot for Discord (2022-2024)

DONE

A 6th player for your League of Legends games, that can actively watch a share screen and point out enemy jungler position or objective timers. You can also address her with Hey Yuumi, and ask a question about builds. Collects data from u.gg API. Only limitation of Yuumi is obviously, she can only attach to one person at a time.
